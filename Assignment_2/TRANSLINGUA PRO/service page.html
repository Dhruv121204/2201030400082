Our Translation Services

At TRANSLINGUA PRO, we offer a comprehensive range of translation services to meet your diverse needs. Our team of expert linguists and translators is dedicated to delivering accurate, reliable, and culturally sensitive translations across various languages and industries.

  • 1. Document Translation

    We specialize in translating a wide variety of documents, including but not limited to:

    • Business documents (contracts, reports, presentations)
    • Academic papers and research articles
    • Legal documents (agreements, certificates, court documents)
    • Medical documents (patient records, research papers, medical reports)
    • Personal documents (letters, resumes, certificates)

  • 2. Website Localization

    Expanding your business to international markets? Our website localization services ensure that your website resonates with your target audience in their native language and culture. From translating website content and user interfaces to adapting graphics and multimedia elements, we help you create a seamless online experience for users worldwide.

  • 3. Software Localization

    Do you have software applications that need to be tailored to different linguistic and cultural contexts? Our software localization experts ensure that your software products are fully functional and linguistically accurate across multiple languages and regions. We handle everything from user interface translation to software testing and quality assurance.
